MAIN SERVICES OVERVIEW

TRACKING AGRICULTURE USING QGIS

We aim to track agricultural production using QGIS , a geospatial software to track Rainfall , Temperature , Humidity , Location and other Crop parameters. We can also get location data which would help farmers to know what crops can be grown and in which area.

CROP PREDICTION USING MACHINE LEARNING

Based on parameters like SEASON, LOCATION , TEMPERATURE , RAINFALL , HUMIDITY , we will predict crops an help farmers to grow the crops which to gain maximum benefit and yield.

SMART FARMING

Smart Farming is an emerging concept that refers to managing farms using modern Information and Communication Technologies to increase the quantity and quality of products while optimizing the human labor required.

PRECISION AGRICULTURE

Precision agriculture (PA) is an approach to farm management that uses information technology ( IT) to ensure that the crops and soil receive exactly what they need for optimum health and productivity. The goal of PA is to ensure profitability, sustainability and protection of the environment. We also help to explain about precision agriculture.

RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT

It is necessary to research about crop parameters and then grow crops. We research all facts for our customers .For over 100 years, our department has led the agriculture industry in world-class research leading to numerous scientific discoveries and the development of new crop varieties grown around the world today.

02

SOIL HEALTH AND PREPARATION

Soil is prepared before sowing the seeds. The soil is loosened to increase the absorption of water and manures. Loosening of soil particles adds humus and nutrients in the soil that increases crop yields. Tilling or loosening the soil is done by ploughs which are pulled by a pair of bulls. We give best techniques to improve soil health to get good yield of crops.

03

TRAITS & TECHNOLOGIES

We propose a farm operation monitoring system using wearable sensor devices with radio frequency identification (RFID) readers and some sensing devices such as motion sensors, cameras, and a GPS. Farm platform makes it possible to monitor the farm constantly, measuring data such as smoke, flooding, air temperature and humidity, the temperature of liquids and power cuts originating from cellars, barns, stables, silos, residential buildings and greenhouses.

04

WEED CONTROL

Weed control is the botanical component of pest control, which attempts to stop weeds, especially noxious weeds, from competing with desired flora and fauna including domesticated plants and livestock, and in natural settings preventing non native species competing with native species. We give good weed control techniques.

05

MICRONUTRIENT FERTILIZERS

Micronutrients include boron, chlorine, copper, iron, manganese, molybdenum and zinc, which are often in short supply for growing crops. Let's take a closer look at the specific role of each micronutrient. Boron: This important nutrient ensures healthy cell growth and assists in the formation of pollen. We provide with all information regarding usage of micronutrient fertilizers.

06

CROP TOLERANCE SCREENS

The varieties to be evaluated or the segregating population to be screened for resistance may be raised in fields under natural infestation, either in endemic areas or by adopting techniques for increasing field infestation. Cultural practices such as closer spacing to create the most desirable humid microclimate within the crop, application of additional dose of nitrogen and irrigation to induce vegetative growth may be adopted.
